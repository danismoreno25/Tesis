The Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in Paraguay was worth 44.46 billion US dollars in 2024, according to official data from the World Bank. The GDP value of Paraguay represents 0.04 percent of the world economy.
source:
World Bank
GDP in Paraguay averaged 14.07 USD Billion from 1965 until 2024, reaching an all time high of 44.46 USD Billion in 2024 and a record low of 0.44 USD Billion in 1965. This page provides - Paraguay GDP - actual values, historical data, forecast, chart, statistics, economic calendar and news. Paraguay GDP - values, historical data and charts - was last updated on  July of 2025.
GDP in Paraguay is expected to reach 46.33 USD Billion by the end of 2025, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Paraguay GDP is projected to trend around 48.50 USD Billion in 2026 and 50.78 USD Billion in 2027, according to our econometric models.
