Chile Producer Prices
Summary
Forecast
Download
Producer Prices in Chile increased to 179.38 points in May from 178.60 points in April of 2025. Producer Prices in Chile averaged 116.95 points from 2012 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 180.98 points in January of 2025 and a record low of 82.41 points in January of 2016.
source:
National Institute of Statistics, Chile
Producer Prices in Chile is expected to be 178.40 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Chile Producer Prices is projected to trend around 197.74 points in 2026 and 206.64 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.
