The Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in Colombia was worth 418.54 billion US dollars in 2024, according to official data from the World Bank. The GDP value of Colombia represents 0.39 percent of the world economy.

GDP in Colombia averaged 121.10 USD Billion from 1960 until 2024, reaching an all time high of 418.54 USD Billion in 2024 and a record low of 4.03 USD Billion in 1960.
