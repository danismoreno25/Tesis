Consumer Price Index CPI in Argentina increased to 8714.49 points in May from 8585.61 points in April of 2025. Consumer Price Index CPI in Argentina averaged 1638.77 points from 2016 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 8714.49 points in May of 2025 and a record low of 100.00 points in December of 2016.
source:
Instituto Nacional de Estadística y Censos (INDEC)
Consumer Price Index CPI in Argentina is expected to be 8903.00 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Argentina Consumer Price Index (CPI) is projected to trend around 12407.00 points in 2026 and 14764.00 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.

Argentina Consumer Price Index (CPI)
In Argentina, the Consumer Price Index or CPI measures changes in the prices paid by consumers for a basket of goods and services. Beginning December of 2013, the Consumer Price Index is published for National Urban Areas, while before only Gran Buenos Aires Area was considered.
