Peru Consumer Price Index (CPI)
Summary
Forecast
Download
Consumer Price Index CPI in Peru increased to 115.66 points in June from 115.51 points in May of 2025. Consumer Price Index CPI in Peru averaged 69.52 points from 1992 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 115.66 points in June of 2025 and a record low of 15.54 points in January of 1992.
source:
Instituto Nacional de Estadística e Informática (INEI)
Consumer Price Index CPI in Peru is expected to be 116.10 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Peru Consumer Price Index (CPI) is projected to trend around 118.78 points in 2026 and 121.16 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.
