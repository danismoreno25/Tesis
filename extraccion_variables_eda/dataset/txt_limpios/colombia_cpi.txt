Colombia Consumer Price Index (CPI)
Summary
Forecast
Download
Consumer Price Index CPI in Colombia increased to 150.14 points in May from 149.66 points in April of 2025. Consumer Price Index CPI in Colombia averaged 32.69 points from 1954 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 150.14 points in May of 2025 and a record low of 0.03 points in March of 1954.
source: Dane, Colombia
Consumer Price Index CPI in Colombia is expected to be 150.93 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Colombia Consumer Price Index (CPI) is projected to trend around 158.06 points in 2026 and 163.59 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.
