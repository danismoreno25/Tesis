The Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in Brazil was worth 2179.41 billion US dollars in 2024, according to official data from the World Bank. The GDP value of Brazil represents 2.05 percent of the world economy.
source:

GDP in Brazil averaged 785.97 USD Billion from 1960 until 2024, reaching an all time high of 2616.16 USD Billion in 2011 and a record low of 17.03 USD Billion in 1960.
