Mexico Producer Prices
Summary
Forecast

Producer Prices in Mexico increased to 133.36 points in May from 133.09 points in April of 2025. Producer Prices in Mexico averaged 83.47 points from 2003 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 133.36 points in May of 2025 and a record low of 48.57 points in December of 2003.
source:
Instituto Nacional de Estadística y Geografía (INEGI)
Producer Prices in Mexico is expected to be 136.21 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Mexico Producer Prices is projected to trend around 144.55 points in 2026 and 149.61 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.
