Consumer Price Index CPI in Paraguay remained unchanged at 138.70 points in June 2025, compared to the prior month. This marks the third consecutive month of flat inflation. Consumer Price Index CPI in Paraguay averaged 96.66 points from 1994 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 155.10 points in December of 2017 and a record low of 32.80 points in December of 1994.
source: Banco Central del Paraguay
Consumer Price Index CPI in Paraguay is expected to be 138.84 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Paraguay Consumer Price Index Cpi is projected to trend around 143.87 points in 2026 and 148.19 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.
