The Gross Domestic Product (GDP) in Ecuador was worth 124.68 billion US dollars in 2024, according to official data from the World Bank. The GDP value of Ecuador represents 0.12 percent of the world economy.

GDP in Ecuador averaged 36.69 USD Billion from 1960 until 2024, reaching an all time high of 124.68 USD Billion in 2024 and a record low of 1.52 USD Billion in 1962.
