Panama Consumer Price Index Cpi
Summary
Forecast
Download
Consumer Price Index CPI in Panama decreased to 110.20 points in May from 110.30 points in April of 2025. Consumer Price Index CPI in Panama averaged 105.57 points from 2014 until 2025, reaching an all time high of 111.12 points in April of 2024 and a record low of 101.42 points in January of 2014.
source:
Instituto Nacional de Estadística y Censo - Panamá
Consumer Price Index CPI in Panama is expected to be 109.12 points by the end of this quarter, according to Trading Economics global macro models and analysts expectations. In the long-term, the Panama Consumer Price Index Cpi is projected to trend around 110.46 points in 2026 and 112.01 points in 2027, according to our econometric models.
